What is ASCENT?

TEDxAJCE 2025 brings the theme ASCENT to life. We explore the journey of overcoming obstacles, achieving greatness, and understanding that every choice we make leaves a permanent mark on our path.

Founded in 2018, TEDxAJCE is an independently organized event that creates a stage where innovation, creativity, and intellectual curiosity meet to share ideas worth spreading.
